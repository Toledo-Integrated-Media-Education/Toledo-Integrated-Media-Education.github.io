About WAKTLP-FM

Community

WAKT is a new non-profit radio station by the Toledo community and for the community.

Free

No commercials, we are funded soley by donations and sponsors.

Content

While we syndicate content, at least 12 hours daily must come from the local community.



How to Give


As a non-profit, we rely heavily on the finacial support of the community. In order for WAKT-LP to be a success, we need your help.

We currently accept donations via PayPal


How to Get Involved

We need help from the community to build for the community

Come to any of our events or contact us directly. We need not only help setting up but are looking for content creators as well.
